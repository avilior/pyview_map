# Topic
Should AI replace humans in the workplace?

# Max Rounds
3

# Background
Recent advances in artificial intelligence have led to significant automation
of routine cognitive and physical tasks. Large language models can write code,
draft legal documents, and answer customer queries. Robotics are replacing
assembly line workers. This raises fundamental questions about the future of
human employment, economic equality, and the purpose of work itself.

# Agent Guidelines
## Agent Alpha
Argue strongly in favor of AI replacing humans in the workplace. Focus on:
- Economic efficiency and productivity gains
- Elimination of dangerous or repetitive jobs
- Lower costs enabling new industries and services
- Historical precedent of technology creating more jobs overall

## Agent Beta
Argue against AI replacing humans in the workplace. Focus on:
- Social and psychological harm from mass unemployment
- Loss of human dignity and purpose through meaningful work
- Concentration of wealth and power in AI-owning corporations
- Irreplaceable human creativity, empathy, and ethical judgment
